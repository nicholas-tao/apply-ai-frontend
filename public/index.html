About ApplyAI

The COVID-19 pandemic has made job hunting extremely difficult and unemployment rates are at an all-time high. ApplyAI makes job hunting quick and easy

  • Find jobs that match your skills and experience
  • Save time and effort by not manually filling in job applications
  • Be notified when new jobs are found

Upload your resume and we will find jobs that match your skills and experience. Select which jobs you are interested in and we will automatically apply to them for you.
